Assignment name  : full_exam_sim
Expected files   : full_exam_sim.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free

Write a program that simulates a simplified exam scoring system. It takes pairs of (exercise_score, max_score) and calculates the total percentage. Display the percentage followed by a newline.

If the number of arguments is even or less than 2, display a newline.

Example:
$> ./full_exam_sim 10 10 8 10 7 10
83
$> ./full_exam_sim | cat -e
$
