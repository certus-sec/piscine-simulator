Assignment name  : final_algorithm
Expected files   : final_algorithm.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free

Write a program that takes a list of integers and finds the maximum sum of any contiguous subarray (Kadane's algorithm). Display the sum followed by a newline.

If the number of arguments is less than 1, display a newline.

Example:
$> ./final_algorithm -2 1 -3 4 -1 2 1 -5 4
6
$> ./final_algorithm | cat -e
$
