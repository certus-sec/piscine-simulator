Assignment name  : pgcd
Expected files   : pgcd.c
Allowed functions: printf, atoi
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a program that displays the greatest common divisor of two numbers
given as arguments, followed by a newline. If the number of arguments is not
2, or if the arguments aren't valid numbers, display 0 followed by a newline.

Example:
$> ./pgcd 42 10
2
$> ./pgcd 42 12
6
