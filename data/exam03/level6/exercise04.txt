Assignment name  : sorting_engine
Expected files   : sorting_engine.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free

Write a program that takes a list of integers and sorts them using quicksort. Display the sorted list separated by spaces, followed by a newline.

If the number of arguments is less than 1, display a newline.

Example:
$> ./sorting_engine 5 2 8 1 9
1 2 5 8 9
$> ./sorting_engine | cat -e
$
