Assignment name  : sorting_advanced
Expected files   : sorting_advanced.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free

Write a program that takes a list of integers and sorts them using merge sort. Display the sorted list separated by spaces, followed by a newline.

If the number of arguments is less than 1, display a newline.

Example:
$> ./sorting_advanced 5 2 8 1 9
1 2 5 8 9
$> ./sorting_advanced | cat -e
$
